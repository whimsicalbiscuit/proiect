Mochi

Mochi is a Japanese rice cake made from glutinous rice that is ground, steamed and pounded into a sticky ball. This pretty, round confection is bite-sized, pillowy soft and often sweetened with sugar and coconut milk. YUM. Try it with green tea for a true Japanese dessert experience.
